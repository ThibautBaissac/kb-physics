# Cliff Cheung

Cliff Cheung is a physicist at Caltech working on [scattering amplitudes](../concepts/scattering-amplitudes.md) and the [bootstrap program](../concepts/bootstrap-method.md).

## Key Contribution

In August 2025, Cheung and collaborators published "Strings From Almost Nothing," demonstrating that [ultrasoftness](../concepts/ultrasoftness.md) — a mathematical property preventing infinitesimal energy concentrations — uniquely yields [string theory](../theories/string-theory.md)'s scattering amplitudes.

Cheung described the reversal of reasoning: "People have studied for decades what string theory implies, and we're asking, 'What implies string theory?'" This reframes the string theory debate from evaluating the theory directly to evaluating whether its foundational assumptions are physically reasonable.
