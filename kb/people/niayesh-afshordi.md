# Niayesh Afshordi

Niayesh Afshordi is an astrophysicist at the University of Waterloo, Canada. His work focuses on observational tests of quantum gravity, including using [gravitational wave detectors](../experiments/gravitational-wave-detectors.md) to probe the structure of black hole horizons and constrain the [no-hair conjecture](../concepts/no-hair-theorem.md).

On the scientific value of testing general relativity near black holes even when the expected deviations may be tiny: "I think it's a worthwhile exercise to go and test that." This captures the empirical spirit behind gravitational wave tests of the no-hair theorem — the constraints are valuable whether they confirm GR or reveal unexpected departures.
