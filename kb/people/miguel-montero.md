# Miguel Montero

Miguel Montero is a physicist at the Institute for Theoretical Physics in Madrid. With [Bruno Bento](../people/bruno-bento.md), he derived the first explicit [de Sitter](../concepts/de-sitter-space.md) solution from [string theory](../theories/string-theory.md).

## Key Contributions

### First Explicit De Sitter Solution (2025)

Montero emphasizes the solution's explicit nature: "We can tell you every detail involved and how it fits together. We can compute a precise value for the dark energy that is close to the exact result." Other physicists given the model "can compute the value of any observable … with precision."

The solution produces dynamical (weakening) dark energy, which Montero connects to recent observations: "If those [DESI] results are here to stay, they are really hinting that the cosmological constant is not a constant."

Starting from M-theory simplified calculations despite adding an extra dimension, and independent confirmation by the Padua group "provided a good check on the general idea."
