# Sarah Porteboeuf

Sarah Porteboeuf served as Run Coordinator for the [ALICE detector](../experiments/alice-detector.md) at CERN during the critical 2023 lead-ion run.

Within the first two minutes of data-taking, Porteboeuf identified an anomaly in the inner tracking system — a "hole" where a quarter of the detector had gone dark. Her quick diagnosis triggered the investigation that ultimately identified lead-207 beam contamination as the culprit, and her coordination of accelerator and detector teams enabled a rapid solution.

Despite losing the first 10 days of the run, ALICE under her coordination collected more data in 2023 than in all prior lead-ion runs combined: "This was an excellent year."
