# Sally Shrapnel

Sally Shrapnel is a physicist at the University of Queensland in Australia, working on quantum foundations.

## Views on Decoherence

Shrapnel has expressed guarded enthusiasm for [Wojciech Zurek](../people/wojciech-zurek.md)'s [decoherence](../concepts/decoherence.md) and [quantum Darwinism](../concepts/quantum-darwinism.md) program, calling it "an elegant approach to explaining the emergence of classicality from the basic postulates of quantum theory." However, she notes it does not address "the thorny question of what the underlying 'quantum substrate' actually is" — the question of how to think about the domain where all quantum possibilities exist before decoherence selects one.
