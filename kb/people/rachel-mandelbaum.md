# Rachel Mandelbaum

Rachel Mandelbaum is an astrophysicist working on weak [gravitational lensing](../concepts/gravitational-lensing.md) and its application to [dark matter](../concepts/dark-matter.md) mapping. She is involved in preparation for the [Vera C. Rubin Observatory](../experiments/vera-c-rubin-observatory.md)'s Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST), emphasizing the importance of community-scale software infrastructure for managing the unprecedented data volumes the survey will produce.

Her view on collaborative science: "We can do better science together." Rubin's LSST will expand weak lensing catalogs from hundreds of millions to billions of galaxy shape measurements — a scale that demands shared pipelines and open data challenges rather than siloed analysis efforts.
