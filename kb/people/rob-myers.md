# Rob Myers

Rob Myers is a physicist at the Perimeter Institute who works on quantum gravity.

## Closed Universe Paradox

Myers highlighted the tension between the mathematical prediction that closed universes have one-dimensional [Hilbert spaces](../concepts/hilbert-space.md) and observable reality: "We look around, and certainly the world seems more complex than that."
