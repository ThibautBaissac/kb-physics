# Renato Renner

Renato Renner is a physicist at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Zurich (ETH Zurich), working on quantum information theory.

## Views on Quantum Foundations

Renner is not persuaded that reconciling the Copenhagen and many-worlds interpretations — as [Wojciech Zurek](../people/wojciech-zurek.md) claims to do via [decoherence](../concepts/decoherence.md) — solves all the problems of the [measurement problem](../open-questions/measurement-problem.md). He points out that it is possible to construct experimentally feasible scenarios in which different observers cannot agree on the outcome of a measurement. Even if such exceptions seem contrived, Renner argues they show that "we've yet to find a quantum interpretation that really works."
