# Marta Volonteri

Marta Volonteri is an astrophysicist at the Paris Institute of Astrophysics.

## Black Holes Before Galaxies

Volonteri studies the co-evolution of black holes and galaxies. Based on JWST discoveries including QSO1 — a naked black hole without a surrounding galaxy in the early universe — she argues for a paradigm shift in understanding cosmic structure formation.

"The most plausible explanation seems to be [that] the black hole developed before the galaxy."

This view challenges the conventional model where galaxies form first and black holes grow within them. The [James Webb Space Telescope](../experiments/james-webb-space-telescope.md) evidence supports the idea that massive black holes may have seeded galaxy formation rather than the reverse — see [Supermassive Black Hole Origins](../open-questions/supermassive-black-hole-origins.md).
