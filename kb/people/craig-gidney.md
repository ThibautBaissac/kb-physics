# Craig Gidney

Craig Gidney is a quantum computing expert at Google Quantum AI.

## Eliminating Complex Numbers from Quantum Computing

In September 2025, Gidney made a significant contribution to the debate over whether [complex numbers](../concepts/complex-numbers-in-quantum-mechanics.md) are essential to quantum mechanics. He approached the question from the perspective of quantum computing, where "logic gates" manipulate quantum bits. One common gate — the T gate — rotates a qubit's state vector around the complex plane, making it the primary source of complex numbers in quantum algorithms.

Gidney found a way to eliminate T gates from any quantum algorithm, numerically proving that quantum computing doesn't require complex numbers. This result, along with independent theoretical work by French and German groups, overturned the 2021 conclusion that complex numbers are essential to quantum theory.
