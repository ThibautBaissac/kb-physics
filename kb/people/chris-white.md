# Chris White

Chris White is a physicist at Queen Mary University of London who, together with his identical twin brother [Martin White](../people/martin-white.md) (University of Adelaide), proposed that the [Large Hadron Collider](../experiments/large-hadron-collider.md) could detect quantum [magic](../concepts/magic-states.md) in entangled top quark pairs.

## Key Contributions

- **Magic at the LHC:** Co-proposed the theoretical framework for measuring magic in top-antitop quark entanglement, providing the foundation for the CMS experiment's first detection of quantum magic in particle physics
- [Regina Demina](../people/regina-demina.md) noted of the twins: "They are identical twins...moved very far apart, but are still in an entangled state"
