# Daniel Harlow

Daniel Harlow is a theoretical physicist at MIT working on quantum gravity, quantum information, and the role of observers in closed universes.

## Observer-Boundary Framework

In 2024-2025, Harlow collaborated with [Ying Zhao](../people/ying-zhao.md) and Mykhaylo Usatyuk to formalize the idea that classical observers restore complexity in closed universes. Building on [Edgar Shaghoulian](../people/edgar-shaghoulian.md)'s proposal, they showed that an observer introduces a boundary around itself (not around the universe), expanding the one-dimensional [Hilbert space](../concepts/hilbert-space.md) and restoring the informational content predicted by observation.

Their January 2025 paper was published alongside independent similar work by another group.
