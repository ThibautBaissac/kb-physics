# Monica Pate

Monica Pate is a theoretical physicist at New York University. She works on the quantum structure of spacetime, including the properties of [de Sitter space](../concepts/de-sitter-space.md) and [cosmological horizons](../concepts/cosmological-horizon.md).

## Key Contributions

Pate has described the fundamental nature of horizons in de Sitter space: "It's expanding so fast that there are parts of space-time which, if you wait forever, you will never be able to see." She also characterized [anti-de Sitter space](../concepts/anti-de-sitter-space.md) as having "a constant gravity everywhere" — a persistent pull that bounds the universe like a box.
