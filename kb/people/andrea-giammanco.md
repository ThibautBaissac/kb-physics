# Andrea Giammanco

Andrea Giammanco is a particle physicist at the Université Catholique de Louvain (Belgium), known for developing [muography](../concepts/muography.md) applications beyond fundamental physics research.

## Key Contributions

Giammanco began exploring how muon imaging could be used outside particle physics R&D. The breadth of applications he discovered — archaeology, volcanology, cargo security, nuclear reactor imaging — "blew my mind," he has said. He and collaborators have investigated using muon scattering tomography to study ancient artefacts and scan cargo containers for illegal goods, exploiting the sensitivity of charged [muons](../concepts/muon.md) to the electric fields of atomic nuclei.
