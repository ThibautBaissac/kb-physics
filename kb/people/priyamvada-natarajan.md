# Priyamvada Natarajan

Priyamvada Natarajan is an astrophysicist at Yale University, working on black hole formation and growth in the early universe.

## Runaway Growth Model

Natarajan, together with Tal Alexander (Weizmann Institute), proposes a scenario for early [supermassive black hole formation](../open-questions/supermassive-black-hole-origins.md): in dense early-universe regions, one star collapses into a large black hole that rapidly consumes surrounding gas and stars. The black hole then persists alone after the other stars exhaust themselves — potentially explaining "naked" black holes like QSO1.

## Views on Primordial Black Holes

Natarajan argues that [primordial black holes](../concepts/primordial-black-holes.md) likely represent only a tiny fraction of dark matter, but that their existence would force reconsideration of alternatives beyond WIMPs and axions.
