# Marcel Vos

Marcel Vos is ATLAS's top quark research leader at the [Large Hadron Collider](../experiments/large-hadron-collider.md), working at the intersection of top quark physics and quantum information.

## Key Contributions

- **Toponium measurements:** Led ATLAS's [toponium](../concepts/toponium.md) measurement (posted July 2025), confirming the top-antitop bound state predicted in 1990 — "was thought to be a too-subtle effect" for LHC detection
- **Entanglement persistence:** Raised the open question of whether [entanglement](../concepts/quantum-entanglement.md) persists through top quark decay: "What happens to your entangled system after the top quark decays? Will the daughters of the top quark still be entangled with the anti-top quark?"
- Described toponium as "our first tangible spin-off from all this" quantum information work

On the field's future: "You start pulling on the thread, and you don't know what you're going to come up with."
