# Thomas Scaffidi

Thomas Scaffidi is a physicist at the University of California, Irvine, working on theoretical aspects of [electron hydrodynamics](../concepts/electron-hydrodynamics.md) and its implications for quantum materials.

## Key Contributions

Scaffidi has collaborated on electron fluid experiments, including work with Andre Geim's group in 2022. He sees geometry-dependent electron flow as a path to new physics: "By using different shapes for your device, you can realize very different physics."

He views electron hydrodynamics as "a baby step" toward using fluid mechanics to understand quantum systems more broadly — describing collective electron behavior through high-level properties like density and viscosity rather than tracking individual particles.
