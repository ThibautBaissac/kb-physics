# Nicole Yunger Halpern

Nicole Yunger Halpern is a physicist working on [quantum thermodynamics](../concepts/quantum-thermodynamics.md).

## Correlations as Thermodynamic Fuel

Yunger Halpern articulated how [quantum entanglement](../concepts/quantum-entanglement.md) enables anomalous heat flow — from cold to hot — by "burning" correlations rather than conventional fuel. In classical refrigeration, work must be done to pump heat against the natural gradient. In the quantum case, entanglement itself serves as the resource: as anomalous heat flow proceeds, particles with correlated properties become independent and the entanglement is destroyed. "Correlations can be used as a resource to push heat in the opposite direction," she explained.
