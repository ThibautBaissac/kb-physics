# Maria Spiropulu

Maria Spiropulu is a physics professor at the California Institute of Technology and co-chair of the committee behind the June 2025 national report endorsing a US muon collider program.

## Key Contributions

### Muon Collider Advocacy

Spiropulu is a leading voice for the proposed [muon collider](../experiments/future-colliders.md), a novel machine that would collide muons — particles 200 times heavier than electrons, yielding both clean and energetic collisions. The major challenge is that muons are highly unstable. She acknowledges the scale of the task: "We have to figure out how to do it in between 10 and 20 billion [dollars]."
