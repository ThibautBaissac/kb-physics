# Flip Tanedo

Flip Tanedo is a particle physicist who has reflected publicly on the current era of null results in particle physics. He draws a historical parallel to the Michelson-Morley experiment of 1887: just as everyone at the time was confident they would detect the luminiferous ether, physicists entering the LHC era were broadly confident they would find [supersymmetric](../concepts/supersymmetry.md) particles. "At the time, it was obvious to everybody that they would detect the ether."

This comparison frames the current moment not as failure but as a potential turning point — the Michelson-Morley null result ultimately led to Einstein's special relativity, one of the most transformative advances in physics.
