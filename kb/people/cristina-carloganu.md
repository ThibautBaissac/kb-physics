# Cristina Carloganu

Cristina Carloganu is a senior researcher at the National Institute of Nuclear and Particle Physics in France (IN2P3/CNRS), working on [muography](../concepts/muography.md) applications including the MURAVES project aimed at imaging the interior of Mount Vesuvius.

## Key Contributions

Carloganu is an advocate for the cross-disciplinary potential of [muon](../concepts/muon.md) research: "Muons are one of the particles that proves that research should not be hyperspecialized. There are many opportunities that have been open by muons — both in looking for new physics, but also in applications."
