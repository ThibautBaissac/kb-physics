# Luca Buoninfante

Luca Buoninfante is a theoretical physicist at Radboud University in the Netherlands whose calculations have helped shore up the case for [quadratic gravity](../theories/quadratic-gravity.md) — the unique [renormalizable](../concepts/renormalization.md) quantum field theory of gravity.

## Key Positions

Buoninfante argues that applying standard [quantum field theory](../theories/quantum-field-theory.md) to gravity yields quadratic gravity uniquely, along with new testable predictions: "So far there is no hint telling us that we should throw quantum field theory away; actually, it's the opposite."

On [ghost particles](../concepts/ghost-particles.md), he maintains they are mathematically consistent: "Mathematically, they make sense now." He views ghosts as "new objects that appear when we try to understand gravity and quantum field theory at a deeper level" — not pathologies to be eliminated but features to be understood.
