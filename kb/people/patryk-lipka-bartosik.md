# Patryk Lipka-Bartosik

Patryk Lipka-Bartosik is a physicist at the Center for Theoretical Physics at the Polish Academy of Sciences, working on practical applications of [quantum thermodynamics](../concepts/quantum-thermodynamics.md).

## Quantum Maxwell's Demon as Catalyst

Lipka-Bartosik realized how to implement a 2004 proposal by Časlav Brukner and [Vlatko Vedral](../people/vlatko-vedral.md) to use thermodynamic properties as [entanglement](../concepts/quantum-entanglement.md) witnesses. His scheme introduces a third system — a quantum Maxwell's demon with quantum memory — that mediates heat flow between correlated hot and cold quantum systems. Because the demon's memory is entangled with both systems, it acts as a catalyst: systematically exploiting all correlations, boosting anomalous heat flow, then returning to its original state.

This work led to the "thermometer for quantumness" paper with [de Oliveira](../people/alexssandre-de-oliveira.md) and Brask, which transformed the catalytic setup into a non-destructive entanglement detector.
