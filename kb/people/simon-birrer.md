# Simon Birrer

Simon Birrer is an astrophysicist at Stanford University specializing in gravitational lensing — in particular the use of strongly lensed [supernovae](../concepts/supernova.md) as probes of the expansion rate of the universe (time-delay cosmology).

Birrer has noted that the [Vera C. Rubin Observatory](../experiments/vera-c-rubin-observatory.md) "will be a game changer" for lensed supernovae research. The LSST is expected to increase the annual detection rate of lensed supernovae by at least 100-fold, reaching approximately 50 per year — dramatically expanding the statistical power of time-delay cosmology measurements.
