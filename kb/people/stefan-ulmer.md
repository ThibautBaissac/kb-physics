# Stefan Ulmer

Stefan Ulmer is the spokesperson of the [BASE experiment](../experiments/base-experiment.md) (Baryon Antibaryon Symmetry Experiment) at CERN. BASE traps antiprotons in Penning traps to make ultra-precise comparisons of proton and antiproton magnetic moments, testing [CPT symmetry](../concepts/cpt-symmetry.md).

## 2025 Antimatter Qubit

Ulmer led the BASE team to the first demonstration of an **antimatter quantum bit** — an antiproton maintained in coherent superposition for 50 seconds, published in *Nature*. On this breakthrough:

> "This represents the first antimatter qubit and opens up the prospect of applying coherent spectroscopy methods to single matter and antimatter systems."

The coherent spectroscopy technique Ulmer's team developed eliminates magnetic field noise that limited prior incoherent measurements, opening a path to 10–100× more precise CPT symmetry tests through the forthcoming BASE-STEP upgrade.
