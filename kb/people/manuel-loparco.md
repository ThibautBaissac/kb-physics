# Manuel Loparco

Manuel Loparco is a physicist at the University of Turin, formerly a graduate student of [João Penedones](../people/joao-penedones.md) at EPFL. He studies quantum field theory in [de Sitter space](../concepts/de-sitter-space.md).

## Key Contributions

### Massive Photons in De Sitter Space (2025)

With Penedones, Loparco showed that massless photons can be composed of massive particles in de Sitter space — a result with strange implications for particle stability and decay in an expanding universe.

### Particle Concept Breakdown

Loparco has emphasized that the very concept of a particle changes in de Sitter space: "In de Sitter, there is no such thing" as a localized particle that moves through space. The constant influx of energy causes particles to spread out or decay.

He views the current state of de Sitter research optimistically: "It's really low-hanging fruit that we're picking."
