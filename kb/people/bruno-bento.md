# Bruno Bento

Bruno Bento is a physicist at the Institute for Theoretical Physics in Madrid. With [Miguel Montero](../people/miguel-montero.md), he derived the first explicit [de Sitter](../concepts/de-sitter-space.md) solution from [string theory](../theories/string-theory.md).

## Key Contributions

### First Explicit De Sitter Solution (2025)

Bento and Montero applied a Casimir-like mechanism to string [compactification](../concepts/compactification.md), balancing the Casimir force (which shrinks the manifold) against flux (which expands it) to produce a small, positive dark energy. They chose Riemann-flat manifolds for the extra dimensions: "It's a simple shape."

The solution is in 5D rather than 4D, which Bento acknowledges as a limitation: "If we cannot find the four-dimensional solution, our work cannot be the final answer."
