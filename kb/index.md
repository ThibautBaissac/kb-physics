# Knowledge Base Index

Master index of all compiled wiki pages. Read this first when answering queries.

## Theories

- [String Theory](theories/string-theory.md) — Framework proposing fundamental particles are vibrating strings in ten dimensions
- [Quantum Field Theory](theories/quantum-field-theory.md) — Theoretical framework combining quantum mechanics and special relativity
- [Standard Model](theories/standard-model.md) — The 25-particle framework governing elementary particles; complete since Higgs discovery but with major gaps
- [Quadratic Gravity](theories/quadratic-gravity.md) — Unique renormalizable QFT of gravity; adds curvature-squared terms, introduces ghost particles, staging a comeback

## Concepts

- [Bootstrap Method](concepts/bootstrap-method.md) — Deriving theories from fundamental principles like symmetry and unitarity
- [Scattering Amplitudes](concepts/scattering-amplitudes.md) — Mathematical objects encoding particle interaction probabilities
- [Supersymmetry](concepts/supersymmetry.md) — Symmetry relating bosons and fermions; N=4 maximal SUSY uniquely leads to string theory
- [Ultrasoftness](concepts/ultrasoftness.md) — Amplitude property preventing energy concentrations, uniquely yields string theory
- [UV Completion](concepts/uv-completion.md) — A theory resolving high-energy infinities of a lower-energy effective theory
- [String Landscape](concepts/string-landscape.md) — The 10^500+ possible string theory configurations
- [Extra Dimensions](concepts/extra-dimensions.md) — Six compactified spatial dimensions required by string theory
- [Black Hole Entropy](concepts/black-hole-entropy.md) — Thermodynamic entropy of black holes, derived microscopically via string theory
- [De Sitter Space](concepts/de-sitter-space.md) — Positively curved spacetime with exponential expansion; the geometry our universe most resembles
- [Anti-de Sitter Space](concepts/anti-de-sitter-space.md) — Negatively curved spacetime acting like a bounded box; enables precise quantum calculations
- [Cosmological Constant](concepts/cosmological-constant.md) — Einstein's Lambda term whose sign determines de Sitter, anti-de Sitter, or flat geometry
- [Cosmological Horizon](concepts/cosmological-horizon.md) — Observer-dependent boundary in de Sitter space beyond which communication is impossible
- [Holography](concepts/holography.md) — Principle that a volume's information is encoded on its boundary; key to black hole and AdS physics
- [Cosmic Inflation](concepts/cosmic-inflation.md) — Period of rapid early-universe expansion approximating de Sitter geometry
- [Decoherence](concepts/decoherence.md) — Environment entanglement destroys quantum superpositions, explaining the quantum-to-classical transition
- [Quantum Darwinism](concepts/quantum-darwinism.md) — Zurek's framework: robust pointer states are naturally selected and amplified into classical reality
- [Quantum Entanglement](concepts/quantum-entanglement.md) — Interacting particles share a single wave function with correlated outcomes regardless of distance
- [Wave Function](concepts/wave-function.md) — Mathematical object encoding all possible measurement outcomes as a quantum superposition
- [Electron Hydrodynamics](concepts/electron-hydrodynamics.md) — Regime where electrons conserve momentum and flow collectively like a fluid
- [Graphene](concepts/graphene.md) — Atomically pure carbon honeycomb enabling observation of electron fluid behavior
- [Hierarchy Problem](concepts/hierarchy-problem.md) — Unexplained disparity between the Higgs mass and the Planck scale
- [Primordial Black Holes](concepts/primordial-black-holes.md) — Early-universe black holes as dark matter candidates, detectable via Hawking radiation
- [Hawking Radiation](concepts/hawking-radiation.md) — Quantum emission causing black holes to evaporate; key to PBH detection
- [Zero-Point Energy](concepts/zero-point-energy.md) — Irreducible ground-state energy of quantum fields; source of the Casimir effect and the vacuum energy puzzle
- [Compactification](concepts/compactification.md) — How string theory's extra dimensions curl up; Casimir approach yields first de Sitter solution
- [Magic States](concepts/magic-states.md) — Quantum states enabling true quantum advantage; first detected in top quark entanglement at the LHC
- [Contextuality](concepts/contextuality.md) — Quantum feature where measurement outcomes depend on co-measured properties; key to magic states
- [Toponium](concepts/toponium.md) — Top-antitop quark bound state, predicted 1990, discovered at LHC 2025 via quantum info methods
- [Page-Wootters Mechanism](concepts/page-wootters-mechanism.md) — 1983 proposal that time emerges from entanglement rather than being fundamental
- [Hilbert Space](concepts/hilbert-space.md) — Abstract mathematical space whose dimensions represent quantum states; closed universes paradoxically have only one dimension
- [Island Formula](concepts/island-formula.md) — Quantum gravity tool tracking information in black hole interiors; reveals closed universes have trivial Hilbert spaces
- [Renormalization](concepts/renormalization.md) — Procedure for removing infinities from QFT by absorbing small-scale effects into measurable constants
- [Ghost Particles](concepts/ghost-particles.md) — Negative-energy particles threatening mathematical consistency; central to quadratic gravity debate
- [Asymptotic Freedom](concepts/asymptotic-freedom.md) — Property where forces weaken at higher energies; discovered in quadratic gravity by Donoghue et al.
- [Complex Numbers in Quantum Mechanics](concepts/complex-numbers-in-quantum-mechanics.md) — Whether imaginary number *i* is essential to QM; 2025 real-valued reformulations show it isn't, but complex arithmetic's hallmarks persist
- [Quantum Thermodynamics](concepts/quantum-thermodynamics.md) — How quantum effects modify classical thermodynamic laws; entanglement enables anomalous heat flow and serves as a non-destructive entanglement detector
- [Quantum Simulation](concepts/quantum-simulation.md) — Using quantum computers or analog quantum systems to simulate quantum fields beyond classical computation limits
- [Qudits](concepts/qudits.md) — Quantum digits with three or more states, enabling more efficient quantum computation than qubits
- [Quantum Chromodynamics](concepts/quantum-chromodynamics.md) — QFT of the strong force; governs quark-gluon binding, largely unsimulatable classically
- [No-Hair Theorem](concepts/no-hair-theorem.md) — Black holes characterized only by mass and spin; any deviations constrained to within 40 km of the horizon by gravitational wave observations
- [QBism](concepts/qbism.md) — Interpretation treating the quantum state as a personal probability catalog for an agent, not an objective reality; quantum mechanics as "a user's manual for the world"
- [Relational Quantum Mechanics](concepts/relational-quantum-mechanics.md) — Rovelli's interpretation where quantum states exist only relative to interactions between systems; no absolute observer-independent description
- [Bell's Theorem](concepts/bells-theorem.md) — Proof that no local hidden-variable theory matches quantum predictions; experimentally confirmed, ruling out local realism
